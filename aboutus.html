About Us

Get to know more about Nkaizen Consulting

Who we are

Nkaizen was started with a mission to offer cost effective world class quality IT solutions to every customer and to translate our solutions into value for our customers. Working together, our team upholds the company’s core values of human value, dignity, commitment and excellence Ensuring that both our clients and candidates are satisfied while getting the great service they deserve.

We are committed to assist clients to implement and optimize the latest software solutions. We ensure our client’s business is well positioned to take advantage of the latest cutting-edge IT technologies as they evolve. We provide consultancy and advice to our clients to enable them to choose the solution most appropriate to their individual needs. We adopt a hands-on approach and work closely with our clients to understand their business and existing resourcing requirements that aligns with client’s strategic business goals.


Why choose us?

Our offers are not limited to technical expertise. Our experts include strategic advisers who analyse organisation’s challenges, and support with comprehensive solutions. Our highly skilled professionals strive towards integration of technology with traditional practices.

Nkaizen Consulting uniqueness lies in

  • Cost efficiency.
  • Effective use of resource.
  • Flexible to market opportunities.
  • Consistency in the service delivery.
  • Increased credibility and competitiveness.
  • Increased productivity and results.
  • Customer satisfaction & improved customer loyalty.
  • Efficient planning & administration processes.

Our Business model focuses on having long-term strategic relations with our clients. In every client project we work beyond the norms of a conventional client relationship by investing more time to understand the client’s current and future Business needs.
